Smart Farm : IOT based soil monitoring

    IoT-based soil monitoring is a smart way to keep track of the health and condition of soil using modern technology.
    It uses small electronic devices called sensors, which are placed in the soil to collect important information like moisture levels (how wet or dry the soil is), temperature, and nutrient content.
    These sensors are connected to the internet, allowing them to send this data to a computer, smartphone, or cloud storage in real-time.
